More about Kasol!

Kasol: A Paradise For Nature Lovers!
Located in Himachal Pradesh, this charming place mesmerizes travellers. The beauty of Kasol hill station is unparalleled. The serenity of places to visit in Kasol attracts tourists from all over the country. It is popularly called the “Mini Israel” of India. Situated at the banks of the Parvati River, this place has still maintained its virginity. And, when it comes to the River Parvati in Kasol, there is no doubt that the tranquil stream is a landmark in itself. Because the river built the valley, everything lovely in Kasol may be attributed to the existence of the Parvati River in the area. Kasol Valley offers opportunities for trekking, camping, rafting, seeing places to visit in Kasol and spiritual awakening. Places to visit in Kasol are ideal for solo travellers, couples, friends and families.

When to Visit Kasol?
Kasol temperature is moderate between March to June. This is the peak season. The days are warm and nights are pleasantly cold. During this time Kasol weather is best for enjoying outdoor activities and exploring the places to visit in Kasol. You will find moderate weather from July to September. Since it is the rainy season, the air is moist and there are possibilities of landslides but the lush green meadows cloaked in the mist will steal your heart! Kasol in winter is extremely cold. It is advisable not to go around the places to visit in Kasol during winters (October to February). Night temperatures fall below zero degree Celsius in Kasol in December and there is a possibility of snowfall.

How to Reach Kasol?
Kullu Manali Airport, known as the Bhuntar airport is the closest airport to Kasol. It is merely 31 km from Kasol. It wont take much time to reach Kasol in a cab before you start seeing places to visit in Kasol. You will find flights from Delhi, Pathankot and Shimla that will drop you at Bhuntar airport. Chandigarh airport is 157 km from Kasol and has better air connectivity. You can take state-run buses from nearby states like Delhi, Haryana and Punjab to reach Kasol. Once you reach here, you need to take a cab to explore places to visit in Kasol. Pathankot, which is 150 km away from Kasol, serves as the nearest railway station and Chandigarh railway station is 310 km away. Private taxis connect these railway stations with Kasol.

Food to Try in Kasol
Though a small place, you will get to experience a huge variety of cuisines at Kasol hill station. You will find a hint of Himachali cuisine, Kumaoni cuisine and Israeli cuisine in Kasol food. Indulge in the delicious Falafel and waffles at the cafes near Manikaran Sahib. Borek, fried onion rings with hummus, Chicken Shipudim with Chips & Hummus and Chicken Laffa are some of the Israeli dishes that are a must-try while exploring places to visit in Kasol. Titillate your taste buds with some lip-smacking momos, egg parathas, Maggi, Thukpa and Tukdiya Bhaat. If you go around the places to visit in Kasol during festivals, you will get to taste Dham, a popular Pahari dish that is mainly prepared by Brahmins of the Chamba region. Kidney beans, pulses and rice are cooked in yoghurt and are teamed up with mash daal, boor ki kari and chutney made from tamarind and jaggery.

Things to Shop in Kasol
The local Kasol market is a paradise for shopaholics. If you are in search of souvenirs after a splendid vacation then this is the right place to be in. You can shop for colourful chillums, bongs and glow-in-the-dark t-shirts. These items are available at a cheap price, and you would be tempted to fill your bags with them. Buy Himachali caps for your loved ones and be sure that they will love these colourful head gears. While on a trip to places to visit in Kasol, pick up intricately woven dreamcatchers, semi-precious stone trinkets, woollen clothes and handicrafts made by local artists. Hone your bargaining skills as you enter this market!

Enjoy a memorable mountain vacation at Kasol. You will love trekking, camping, festivals and parties. Enjoy bliss in the midst of nature at the splendid Kasol.

Here are a few quick facts to make your travel safe and easy to Kasol. Kasol is a Hills & Mountains destination. The best time to visit Kasol is March, April, May and June. Since this is the peak season expect a little crowd during this time. July, August and September period experiences moderate weather. So, you can easily avoid facing a large gathering.
In peak season the weather is warm days and cool nights, whereas in off-season the weather is intense cold with night temperatures falling below freezing point. Weather in moderate season is damp weather with possible landslides. During Badoh Mela, Fagdi Mela, a large number of tourists flock together. The festivals are celebrated in the month of August, February,... consecutively. So, if you are fond of bustling streets and localities, choose this time of the year to travel.
If your travel seems incomplete without getting into some fun activities then you will love to get indulged in trekking, camping, festivals and parties. In moderate season you will love to get indulged in lush green landscapes cloaked in mist.

Conclusion

Rediscover life as you spend time with yourself amidst nature at the places to visit in Kasol. Also known as the “Amsterdam of India”, this place is one of the hidden gems of Himachal that is tucked under the folds of greenery and is still untouched by urbanisation. It is a popular joint for trekkers, backpackers and adventurers who throng this valley to indulge in popular adventure sports. So, pack your bags and let yourself loose at the Kasol places to visit and make your trip memorable!
